Common Questions
Frequently Asked
How long does delivery take? ›
We dispatch all orders within 24 hours on business days. Nairobi deliveries typically arrive within 1–2 days. Upcountry Kenya takes 3–5 days. East Africa shipping is 5–10 business days depending on location.
Do you offer wholesale pricing? ›
Yes! We offer competitive wholesale pricing for restaurants, retailers, caterers, and event organizers. Minimum order quantity is 10 units per SKU. Contact us via the Wholesale topic above or email wholesale@savoraspices.co.ke for our full price list.
Are your spices really organic? ›
Our Certified Organic range carries full third-party certification. All other products are 100% natural with no additives, artificial colours, or preservatives — we just don't use the organic label unless the farm certification is in place. Every product page clearly indicates certification status.
Can I return a product? ›
We accept returns within 14 days of delivery if the product is unopened and in original packaging. If you received a damaged or incorrect item, we'll replace it immediately at no cost. Please contact us with a photo of the issue for fastest resolution.
Where do you ship to? ›
We currently ship across Kenya (all 47 counties), Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, and Ethiopia. We're actively working on expanding to more African markets. International shipping outside East Africa is available on request — contact us for a quote.
How should I store my spices? ›
Store spices in a cool, dark, dry location — away from direct sunlight, heat, and moisture. Our zip-seal pouches are designed to maintain freshness between uses. Whole spices last up to 3–4 years; ground spices are best within 1–2 years of opening.

Our Highland Turmeric is sourced exclusively from small family farms nestled in Kerala's Western Ghats, at elevations above 1,200 metres. The cooler climate and volcanic soil produce a rhizome with significantly higher curcumin content than lowland varieties — meaning richer colour, more potent flavour, and greater health benefits in every pinch.

Cold-stone ground and packed immediately after milling to preserve its volatile oils, Savora Turmeric transforms curries into something spectacular — a deep, warm golden hue that no synthetic colouring can replicate.

Best used in: Curries, golden milk, rice dishes, marinades, soups, and smoothies.

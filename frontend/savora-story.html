The Founder

A Kitchen, a Dream,
and a Suitcase Full of Spices

It started in 2018, when Amara Odhiambo returned from a year travelling through India, Sri Lanka, and Morocco. She came home with journals full of recipes, a deep tan — and six kilos of spices she'd collected from farms, markets, and roadside stalls across the world.

Back in Nairobi, she cooked for friends and family, and the reaction was always the same: "What is in this? I've never tasted anything like it." She realised the difference wasn't her cooking — it was the quality of the spices. And that quality simply wasn't available to ordinary people in Kenya.

So she went back. She built relationships with farmers, learned about terroir, curcumin content, and essential oil profiles. In 2019, Savora Spices was born — with a mission to make extraordinary spices accessible to every home cook.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Six principles that guide every decision we make — from the farms we partner with to the packaging we choose.

🌱
Ethical Sourcing

We only work with farms that pay fair wages, respect the land, and use sustainable growing practices. Every purchase supports the livelihoods of real farming families.

🔬
Uncompromising Quality

Every batch is lab-tested for purity, essential oil content, and freedom from additives. If it doesn't meet our standard, it doesn't reach your kitchen.

🌍
Global Curiosity

We travel to find flavours the world hasn't discovered yet — from highland farms in Kerala to remote co-operatives in the Atlas Mountains of Morocco.

🤝
Community First

10% of every sale goes back to the farming communities we source from — funding schools, clean water access, and agricultural training programmes.

♻️
Sustainable Packaging

All our packaging is 100% recyclable or compostable. We're working toward a fully plastic-free supply chain by 2027.

🍽️
Flavour Education

We believe everyone deserves to cook brilliantly. That's why every product ships with usage guides, recipes, and pairing suggestions.
